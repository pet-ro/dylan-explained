devide and conquer of software engineering 
  Abstaction and modularization are the `divide and conquer`
  of software engineering. The `devide and conquer` process
  of software engineering goes along with changing 
  the granularity of information hiding and information 
  exposing with each new abstraction level. 
  More details are given in the entries 
  :term:`abstraction` and :term:`modularization`.
