the compiler
------------
  
  The compiler described here is **Open Dylan**, formerly named Dylan- 
  Works and Functional Developer. 

  - the compiler was originally developed at Harlequin 
    as *DylanWork*, 
  - later Functional Objects took it over and named the compiler
    *Functional Developer*, 
  - since March 2004 the compiler is open source and got named
    *Open Dylan*

.. _dylanwork-the-compiler(focus):

  Harlequin's DylanWork consists of a Compiler and IDE.
  We will focus here on the compiler.

.. _dylanwork-the-compiler:

  We use here the phrase **the dylanwork compiler** as a reminiscence to
  its main contributor Harlequin. 
  
  This naming convention is also used in a Diploma Thesis related to
  OpenDylan: [Diploma thesis Mehnert (2009)]_
