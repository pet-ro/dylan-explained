about Dylan 
-----------

  The dylan language was designed in the early nineties. 
  A huge group of skilled people were involved:
  
  - The :drm:`Colophon@DRM <Colophon>` lists all contributors to 
    the dylan language design. 

  The goal was:
  
  -  to design - with Lisp im mind - a new mainstream language.

  Financial crisis of its main supporters kill the launching process 
  of the Language. Its main supports were:
 
  -  Apple and 
  -  Harlequin 
 
  Details are given in
  
  - `History of Dylan Programming Language@wikipedia`_.

  We, the OpenDylan Community, feel 
  
  - the dylan language its design is still inspiring  
   
    - even after so many years. 

  - the dylan compiler design by Harlequin is impressing

    - even after so many years

  We are looking for contributors with an interest in

  - modern language features
  - compiler design

  provide this glossary.

  <dylan-term-about-dylan>`
  introduces the glossary.
